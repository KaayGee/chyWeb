About Chy Foundation

Over the years,I have had the feeling that it would be great and fruitful to establish a charity organization. My passion for giving and imparting lives motivated me to this foundation on 14th May 2020. Within 4 months of launching this progragram and while COVID-19 struck the globe we have reached out to beggers and children on the street and inmates at Nigeria correctional centre located at Nnewi, Anambra state, Nigeria. We have also reached out to about 500 persons and counting through our facebook. channel. I hope to continue this journey of giving and with the right sponsors, we will be able to reach the target needy in the near future.

Mission

To meet the needs of the needy wherever they may be found around the world, be it rural or urban area.

Our Founder

Chioma Nwosu Onyekaba is a Nigerian-Irish philanthropist currently based in Ireland. She is from Uruagu, Nnewi North, Anambra state. Chioma is married with kids, all residing in Ireland. Her passion for giving motivated her to establish Chioma Giving Foundation.

Vision

To eradicate homelessness and work towards a greener world through empowerment for self reliance.

Support Our Movement

There is tremendous amount of satisfaction in helping the needy and equipping them. Together we can work as facilitators of self-reliance. Together we can strengthen families and by extention the large communities.
